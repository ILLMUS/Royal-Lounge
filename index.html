About 23:28

23:28 Lounge is a trendy, vibrant bar and restaurant in Mbabane, situated in the Checkers Business Park. It’s celebrated for its modern chic décor blended with local artistic flair. The lounge caters to both locals and tourists, offering an inviting, lively ambiance ideal for both casual relaxation and nighttime socializing.

At 23:28, we believe in making every moment special, whether it's a lunch break or gathering with friends.
